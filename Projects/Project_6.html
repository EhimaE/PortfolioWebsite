Festive Cup

2024

Design project that I made of a coffee cup design. I first compiled images into a mood board and made rough sketches on the ideas. I made it in photoshop, and this is the result.

This was the final design I landed on. The main theme of this pink and blue design was to be reminiscent of a winter sweater. On the bottom left there are two extra patterns I came up with.

These ones are extra coffee cup designs that came with it. The top cup is supposed to be a scene of a cabin in the winter and the second one is to be like a snowflake pattern.
