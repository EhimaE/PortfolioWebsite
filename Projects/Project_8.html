Self Branded Magazine

2022

A project where I had to create a magazine that represented myself. I made mood boards on the style and layout I was interested in and then after experimenting with different layouts landed on this idea.

These are the first two page the sketch was based on a photo of the landscape. Then a circle was placed in the centre to unify the pages.

For the secound set of pages I wanted to go for a darker background to contrast the first and last sets of pages. For the text I used white font instead so that it would stand out.

The last set of pages is definitely my favourite one I got a photo and added different digital effect to get the image I had the image spill over onto the other page just to keep the sense of unity between the two.
