Owl Faces

2023

A personal project where I wanted to create Logo like images of owls and then engrave it onto the cover of a sketchbook. I made multiple sketches first and then designed it digitally once I got a design that I liked I engraved it onto the book.

This is the digital version of the logo. I used vectors to get the smooth lines and then I used the symmetry tool to get the final look.

Here is the rough visualization of how the logos would look like on my sketchbook cover, I did this to test out what composition that I would like before painting it on physically..
